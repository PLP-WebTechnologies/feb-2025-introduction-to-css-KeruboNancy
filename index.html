Why Go Mango

Ah, the mango, the fruit that thinks it's a comedian. With its slippery peel always trying to pull a fast one on you, it’s like nature’s way of saying, “Hey, life’s messy, deal with it!” You slice into one, and suddenly your kitchen looks like a crime scene from a juice heist. And don’t even get me started on those stubborn pits; they’re basically the bouncers of the fruit world, refusing to let you enjoy the goods without a fight. Yet despite all this chaos, mangos have us wrapped around their sticky little fingers because, let’s face it, once you taste that sweet, tropical nectar, you’ll forgive them for everything—even the time they stained your favorite white shirt.
